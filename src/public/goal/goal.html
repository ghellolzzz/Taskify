What Are SMART Goals?

SMART Goals help you write clear, achievable goals. Use this checklist:

  • S — Specific: Clearly define the goal.
  • M — Measurable: How will you track progress?
  • A — Achievable: Is it realistic and reachable?
  • R — Relevant: Does it align with your long-term plans?
  • T — Time-bound: Set a clear deadline.
Example:
“Save $500 by June 2025 by putting aside $50 every week.”
